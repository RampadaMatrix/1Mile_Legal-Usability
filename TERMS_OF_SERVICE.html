What 1Mile Provides

1Mile provides local information utilities for Purulia, including:

  • Weather forecasts and Bengali local-weather insight
  • Transit discovery, route search, journey planning, nearby bus estimates, and community-built bus data
  • Bengali calendar and Panjika information
  • Healthcare directory information such as doctors, hospitals, emergency numbers, and one-tap dialer actions
  • A frozen Local Medicine interest surface that may collect interest for a possible future service
  • AI-assisted and manual bus contribution tools

The app is informational and community-powered. It is not an official government, transport authority, bus operator, hospital, pharmacy, or emergency-response service unless a specific official relationship is expressly stated.

Database Coverage and Scope

Currently, our database projects and supports only 2,626 villages from the Purulia district. Hyperlocal features, map projections, directory services, and transit calculations are limited to this covered scope. Data for other districts, regions, or additional villages is not available at this time.

Transit Estimates Are Not Real-Time Tracking

1Mile does not track buses in real time. The app does not receive live GPS locations from buses or operators.

Bus routes, stop times, "next arrival" labels, journey plans, status labels, and map results are estimates generated from community contributions, past data, timetable or route-board images submitted by users, route geometry, cached data, and algorithmic calculations.

Villages, stops, route lines, route shapes, discovery points, and bus paths may be projected onto Google Maps by 1Mile using community data, local datasets, and algorithmic calculation. These projected map points are not necessarily native Google Maps place data, official transport-authority data, or true verified ground locations.

Google Maps may provide the base map and map display technology, but Google is not responsible for 1Mile's projected village, stop, route, timing, or bus-location disparities. If you find a village, stop, route point, or bus path projected incorrectly on the map, contact us immediately through the official support email and WhatsApp support channel so we can review and correct it.

Under normal circumstances, a bus estimate may differ by about ±30 minutes. The difference may be greater because of road conditions, traffic, weather, festivals, market days, mechanical issues, operator decisions, missing data, bad source photos, route changes, no trip on that day, a bus not being in service that day, temporary suspension, or permanent discontinuation of a bus service.

You are responsible for verifying important travel locally, leaving a buffer, and not relying on 1Mile as the sole source for time-critical travel.

Bus Contributions And Edits

The bus map grows because riders and local contributors add and correct information. When you contribute, you may use a route-board photo, timetable photo, handwritten route list, AI extraction, manual drafting, route plotting, HITL review, duplicate shield, publish preferences, and edit tools for existing buses.

When you add a new bus or edit an existing bus, including by using a bus-name search/edit flow, you are responsible for:

  • Submitting only information you believe is true and useful
  • Checking AI-extracted drafts before plotting or publishing
  • Verifying the correct bus, direction, registration number, route, stops, and timing before changing an existing record
  • Checking projected stop, village, and route-map positions before submitting or publishing route geometry
  • Avoiding accidental edits to a different bus with the same or similar name
  • Not uploading private personal data, faces, medical documents, unrelated images, abusive content, or copyrighted content you are not allowed to share
  • Correcting mistakes promptly when you discover them

We reserve the right to edit or remove any bus or transit information contributed by a user. Specifically, we may review, reject, correct, hide, remove, merge, preserve, or roll back bus contributions when needed to protect riders, prevent duplicates, fix errors, investigate abuse, maintain audit history, or comply with law.
