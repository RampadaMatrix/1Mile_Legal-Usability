1. Summary

Most of 1Mile can be used without signing in. Google sign-in is used mainly for bus contribution, route editing, profile, and account-linked actions. The app uses foreground location only when you request nearby transit discovery. The app does not track buses in real time and does not track user location in the background.

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data.

2. Data We Collect Or Access

Account And Sign-In Data

When you sign in with Google, we may receive and store account identifiers such as your user ID and email address. We use this to identify bus contributors, manage contribution history, prevent abuse, and support account features.

Transit Contribution Data

When you add or edit bus data, we may collect and store:

  • Contributor user ID and account-linked metadata
  • Contributor name, email, and avatar/display data when provided by account/profile systems
  • Bus name, registration number, route summary, direction, origin, destination, stops, stop order, timings, coordinates, route geometry, and route polyline
  • Algorithmically projected village, stop, route, path, and map-coordinate data used to display routes on Google Maps
  • Contribution ID, draft ID, app version, local master data version, publish preference, status, review notes, changed fields, timestamps, reviewer comments, duplicate checks, and audit history
  • AI extraction chat/session metadata and route draft data

Some of this contribution data may become visible to other users as part of the public community transit map, search results, bus cards, contributor credits, or editor history.

Photos And Images You Select

If you choose to provide an image, we may process it for the selected feature:

  • Route board, timetable, or handwritten route-list images may be compressed and sent to the transit pipeline or AI route extraction service to create an editable draft.
  • Profile pictures may be uploaded and stored for profile display.

Only upload images that are relevant to the feature and that you have the right to share. Avoid including people, private personal data, medical documents, financial data, or unrelated content.

Location Data

If you tap the Location feature in Transit, the app asks for foreground fine or coarse location permission. The app uses your current location to set a map discovery point and find nearby buses or corridors.

1Mile does not use background location permission and does not use your device location to track buses.

Google Maps may be used as the base map. The stop, village, route, bus-path, and discovery overlays shown by 1Mile are generated from 1Mile/community data and algorithmic projection. They are not necessarily native Google Maps locations or true verified ground positions. If you find a village or stop projected incorrectly, contact the official support email and WhatsApp support channel immediately so the map data can be reviewed.

Weather, Calendar, And Local Data

Weather and calendar features use Purulia-focused data and app caches. Weather is for Purulia and does not require your personal location. The app may cache weather, calendar, village, transit, and route data locally to improve performance and offline behavior.

Local Medicine Interest

Medicine delivery operations are currently frozen. If you choose to register interest for a possible future local medicine service while signed in, we may store your user ID, email, profile name, phone number, status, source, timestamps, and optional note.

Technical And Diagnostic Data

We may process app version, local data version, service status, cache status, request status, errors, contribution status, and basic logs to operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.

3. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide app features such as transit discovery, weather, calendar, healthcare directory, profile, and contribution history
  • Authenticate signed-in users
  • Attribute bus contributions and route edits
  • Prevent duplicate, abusive, false, or low-quality route data
  • Process route images and AI-assisted drafts
  • Review, publish, correct, or remove bus contributions
  • Show nearby buses when you request location-based discovery
  • Record local medicine interest when you request it
  • Maintain service security, troubleshoot errors, and comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing And Service Providers

We may use service providers to run the app, including:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, profile, contribution, storage, and app backend data
  • Google services — Google sign-in, Google Maps base-map display, and device location APIs. Google is not responsible for 1Mile's community or algorithmically projected stop, village, route, or bus-path locations.
  • 1Mile weather backend — Purulia weather
  • 1Mile transit pipeline and AI route extraction service — contribution processing
  • Device apps selected by you — such as the phone dialer when you tap a call button

These providers may process data only as needed to support app functionality, security, legal compliance, or service operation. We do not sell personal or sensitive user data.

We may disclose data if required by law, to respond to valid legal requests, to protect users, to investigate abuse, to enforce our Terms, or as part of a lawful organizational change with appropriate notice.

5. Transit Data Visibility

Bus route facts submitted through contributions may become public within the app because the community transit map depends on shared route knowledge. This can include bus name, route, stops, timing estimates, direction, route geometry, contributor credits, editor counts, and audit-derived metadata.

Do not submit information that you do not want used for the community transit service.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described above, including:

  • Account/session data while your account is active
  • Profile data until updated, anonymized, deleted, or no longer needed
  • Local app cache until replaced, cleared, or removed by uninstall/device clearing
  • Contribution records while needed for transit integrity, audit, duplicate prevention, dispute handling, abuse prevention, security, or legal compliance
  • Published route facts while useful to riders and the community transit map

7. Account Deletion

The app includes an account deletion option. When used, the current implementation anonymizes your personal profile and clears your local session. Published route facts and limited contribution audit records may be retained where needed for community transit integrity, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.
